Welcome To HTU

Al Hussein Technical University, established by the Crown Princ Foundation, was born out of a need to bridge the gap between the numbers of graduates and their rate of employment in technical fields. To remedy this problem, the Crown Prince Foundationsought to create a distinguished model of technical education that features a seamles combination of excellence, rigor and close attunement to the ever-changing demands and developments of Indusliy .

Why HTU

Al Hussein Technical University (HTU) is a higher education institution established to support Jordanian youth’s pursuit of vocational, technical and applied education. As a liue advocate of STEM education, HTU works to achieve a world-leading education for young people in science, technology, engineering and mathematic (STEM) . The university offers diploma and bachelor degree programs in the fields of applied engineering. Our hands-on curriculum is informed by indusliial needs, local and global challenges. It is designed to spur innovative, problem solving, and enliepreneurial spirit among our students .
